Rexburg is Where Adventures Begin!

Rexburg is a great place to work, live and raise a family. Rexburg keeps its welcoming small town feel while it continues to grow. Rexburg is the gateway to both Grand Teton and Yellowstone National Parks, Teton, Big Hole Mountain Ranges, and surrounded by world class hunting and fishing grounds. With Sun Valley to the west and Jackson Hole, Wyoming to the east, Rexburg is the natural starting point for an Eastern Idaho Adventure. If you are looking for a place that focuses on comunity and helping one another then Rexburg is where you want to be, and if you are looking to support or build local businesses then consider joining the Rexburg chamber of commerce.
