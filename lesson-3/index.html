Site Name and Purpose

The site name is going to be "Rexburg Events". I want to create a site that will house not just the weather for the local area but also have up coming city events so so students and families can plan outings. There are plenty of things that go on in Rexburg but people don't hear about them. Along with the events there will also be local news to keep people informed about important things happening in our community.
