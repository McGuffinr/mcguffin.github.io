Audience

The main audience would be families looking for things to do in Rexburg and surrounding area.

Persona 1

A parent who is looking for fun events to take the family to and stay connected through local news stories.

Persona 2

A new student who doesn't know what Rexburg has to offer.

Scenario 1

A parent has the upcoming weekend off and wants to make the most of their time with the family. They check rexburgcityevents.com to see what they can take the family to and see what the weather will be like for their outing.

Scenario 2

Its a students first semester at BYU-I and they want to know about some upcoming events so they can go out and meet some new people.

Scenario 3

A father is wanting to take is son on a camping trip but needs to check what the weather will be like.

Scenario 4

There is an up coming election and people want to know what is going on in local politics so they can decide who they think the new Mayor should be.

Scenario

A couple is getting married and is getting married outside. They want to see what the weather will be like so they can plan accordingly.
